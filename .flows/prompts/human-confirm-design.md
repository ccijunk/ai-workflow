# Human Confirmation - Design

Review the design and test design documents.

## Your Task

1. Read the design document: `docs/design.md`
2. Read the test design document: `docs/test-design.md`
3. Decide whether to approve or reject the design

## Decision

If the design is acceptable:
- Write "yes" to `design-approved.txt`

If the design needs re-work:
- Write "no" to `design-approved.txt`
- Add comments about what needs to be fixed

## Output

Write your decision to `design-approved.txt` (yes or no).
